About Rapid Technologies

Rapid Technologies is your leading managed service provider between Fresno and Bakersfield, California. We offer phone, remote, and onsite support during regular business hours with immediate emergency support on standby 24 / 7 / 365.

From standard, repetitive tasks to large scale project implementation, we rely on the Autotask Service Desk application to manage every IT aspect through to completion. Along with the Autotask Endpoint Management remote monitoring and management tool, we also ensure that your data is protected using Webroot enterprise and internet security. Often, we can assess and fix your problem remotely in minutes, saving the time and cost of sending a technician to you. When something does go wrong, we are experts in data recovery and business continuity using Datto, Gilware, and Carbonite solutions.
